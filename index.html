Ben 10: Pet Guardians

How to Play

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Mouse Click
  • Open Omnitrix: Q
  • Select Alien: Number Keys 1-5

Controls:

  • Move: Virtual Joystick
  • Shoot: Attack Button
  • Transform: Omnitrix Button

Objective:

Survive waves of enemies, collect power-ups, and save pets!
